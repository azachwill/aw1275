**Who should go?**

[rstudio::conf](https://rstd.io/conf) is for RStudio users, R administrators, and RStudio partners who want to learn how to write better Shiny applications, explore all the capabilities of R Markdown, work effectively with Spark or TensorFlow, build predictive models, understand the tidyverse of tools for data science, build tidy tools themselves, discover production-ready development & deployment practices, earn certification as a trainer for Shiny or the Tidyverse, or become a certified administrator of RStudio professional products.

**Why do people go to rstudio::conf?**

Because there is simply no better way to learn about all things R & RStudio.
